ABOUT US

Lifeflash Clinic is a private health Clinic in Kiryandongo District (Uganda).
It offers quality Services in all cases. The Clinic's aim is to provide Medicial
care Services to people that will come for the Offer, in order to reduce
mortality and morbidity in their communities and
the nation at large.

Lifeflash Clinic is located in the rural town of Bweyale in Kiryandongo
district in mid-western Uganda, about 220 km from the capital
city Kampala, opened its doors in November 2019 with
numbers of patients in the Clinic.
